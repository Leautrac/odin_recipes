Lasagna

Homemade and delicious lasagna

This lasagna is made with zucchini slices instead of pasta. This low-carb, gluten-free beef lasagna
is delicious and satisfying; it's a crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Minced Meat
  • Onions
  • Pasta for lasagna
  • Milk & nutmeg
  • Starch

Steps:

  1. Heat a sauce pan with olive oil inside
  2. Brown onions in
  3. Pour & stir the minced meat to slightly cook it
  4. Pour tomato sauce and let it reduce on low heat
  5. Heat milk with nutmeg, pour starch in at boiling point, reduce fire and stir till thickened
  6. First spread pasta slices in a dish, then tomatoes sauce then bechamel etc, and voila!!!
